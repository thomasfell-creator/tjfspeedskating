Speed Skating 101

A beginner's guide to learning Speed Skating.

Welcome to Speed Skating 101, a free learning resource created to help skaters, parents, and coaches understand the fundamentals of speed skating.

Whether you're stepping onto the ice for the first time or looking to improve your technique, this series provides clear explanations, practical drills, and structured training plans developed from real coaching experience.

Speed skating is one of the most technically demanding sports in the world. Success is built upon mastering a series of fundamental skills that develop over time. The purpose of Speed Skating 101 is to break those skills down into simple, easy-to-follow lessons.

Each section covers::

  • The key principles of the skill
  • Common mistakes and how to fix them
  • Off-ice exercises
  • Coaching tips

I suggest starting with Part 1: Basic Position before progressing through each section in order. However, each unit will be available below as they are developed.
